Resume

Objective: Piano, Singing, Theory

Highlights of Qualifications and Achievements:

  • Member of ORMTA (Ontario Registered Music Teachers Association) since 2003.
  • Taught piano in Russia since 1989 and Canada since 2003 to children and adults, all grades, with successful results from students taking RCM examinations.
  • Performed as a Concertmaster since 1989.
  • Performed as solo classical concert pianist (Chopin/Liszt/Schumann) several times since 2004 at the Rogers Theatre, Mississauga Living Arts Centre, Ontario, Canada.
  • Vocal teacher since 2003 years with children aged five to nineteen.
  • Graduated with distinction as teacher of music and concertmaster, Methodist of musical education, teacher of music disciplines in pedagogic school: musical instrument piano, conductor-choral and historical theoretical ones.
  • Director of dancing group in school and non-school institutions.
  • Director of optional course in history of fine arts.
  • Director of study group specializing in painting.
